About Naresh & Co

NARESH AND CO offers end-to-end engineering solutions, construction, installation and commissioning of telecom wireless and wireline equipment's and networks.

We specialize in comprehensive telecom infrastructure solutions including Survey, Installation and Commissioning of all Telecom Equipment's, IBS Poles and Micro Towers (up to 16 Meters), Macro Towers up to 55 meters (GPT and RTT), and deployment of 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G, MW and Microwave hops.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified: We are proud to be ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring that all our work follows international quality management standards. This certification demonstrates our commitment to delivering consistent, high-quality services and continuous improvement in all our telecom infrastructure projects.

Our commitment to the telecom sector is demonstrated by our substantial skills and investment in tools and training on the latest technologies, enabling us to execute projects with the highest level of efficiency. We have specific customized solutions and a highly trained team of experts specialized in execution of telecom contracts with the ability to respond to the most demanding challenges.

Our valued customers include: Bharti Airtel Ltd (Pan India operations), Huawei Telecommunications India Pvt Ltd, Tech Mahindra Ltd, and Data Dimension.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for telecommunication infrastructure

📡

Telecom Infrastructure Services

Complete telecom infrastructure solutions including site survey (BTS/LOS/IBS), foundation works, tower erection, power equipment installation, GSM/CDMA BTS installation & commissioning, de-installation & upgrading, microwave hops alignment, and MSC/BSC/STP equipment installation.

⚡

Transmission & Distribution

Complete T&D services including transmission line surveys, route alignment, tower spotting, foundation & tower protection works, tower earthing & erection, conductor stringing, OPGW and earth wire stringing (including live line), and final checking & commissioning.

🔍

Inspection Services

Comprehensive inspection services for transmission line materials, towers, conductors, OPGW, hardware, accessories, earthing materials, site inspections, foundation works, tower erection inspection, stringing inspection, and complete site auditing.

🌐

Network Deployment & Integration

Deployment of 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G, MW and Microwave hops. WIFI, IWAN, FTTH and FTTB Network Design and Installation with complete configuration and commissioning services.

🔌

Wire Line Services

Installation and Commissioning of MSAN Equipment's, Switches, MUX and Routers. OFC and Copper network deployment, voice and DSL migration, and MDF termination services.

📋

Project Management Services

End-to-end project management from site survey to integration. Design review & approvals, scheduling, procurement management, site implementation, quality assurance, OHSE compliance, scope management, and statutory clearance assistance.
