About Naresh & Co

Naresh & Co is a trusted name in the telecommunication infrastructure industry, specializing in comprehensive electrical engineering solutions and turnkey EPC services.

Based in Chennai, we deliver end-to-end solutions for telecommunication tower infrastructure with proven expertise in assembling and erecting large-scale steel lattice towers. Our recent projects include successful completion of 25-meter, 55-ton radar tower installations for the Royal Thai Air Force, demonstrating our capability to handle complex international projects with precision and adherence to stringent safety standards.

Our team of certified riggers, quality engineers, and HSE officers ensures every project meets the highest standards of structural integrity, safety compliance, and operational excellence.
